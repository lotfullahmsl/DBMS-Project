Our Journey

Lotfullah Muslimwal and Muhammad Hamza met at a major footwear retailer, frustrated by the lack of innovation in online shoe shopping. In 2025, they combined their skills to create SoleNest, building a fast, reliable, and user-friendly platform.

The Vision

To revolutionize online footwear retail by combining cutting-edge technology with curated style selections and exceptional customer service.

The Mission

Help customers find their perfect pair of shoes through an intuitive platform that makes online shoe shopping enjoyable, personalized, and hassle-free.
